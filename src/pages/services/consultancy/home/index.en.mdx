## Our Service Approach

At ARTEK, we address your technical consultancy needs with our engineering vision.

We guide industrial enterprises on technical matters by combining our expertise in *mechanical engineering*, *industrial engineering*, and *computer engineering* with academic experience. We do not provide financial advisory, legal consultancy, or auditing services — we focus on **technical processes that require engineering knowledge**.

With the critical advantage of **focused expertise** that this approach provides, we deliver in-depth services on technical matters requiring engineering knowledge.

## What We Do

### <Checkmark size={20} /> Technical Project Evaluation

We analyze the technical feasibility, R&D characteristics, and innovation aspects of your projects from an engineering perspective. We evaluate technological uncertainty, originality, and applicability criteria.

### <Checkmark size={20} /> Publicly Funded Project Consultancy

We provide technical guidance for project preparation, application, and management processes for support programs from TÜBİTAK, KOSGEB, Development Agencies, and other public institutions.

### <Checkmark size={20} /> R&D and Design Center Consultancy

We provide consultancy on the technical aspects of establishing R&D or Design Centers under Law No. 5746. We assist with project portfolio, personnel competency, and physical infrastructure planning.

### <Checkmark size={20} /> Process and System Design

We provide engineering support for the design and optimization of production processes, quality management systems, project management infrastructure, and technical documentation systems.

### <Checkmark size={20} /> Technical Training and Capacity Building

We organize corporate training programs on R&D management, project documentation, technical writing, engineering processes, and digital tools.

### <Checkmark size={20} /> University-Industry Collaboration Coordination

Through our academic connections, we facilitate your introduction to partners who can provide academic support for your projects, help you develop joint R&D projects, and enable you to benefit from research infrastructures.

### <Checkmark size={20} /> Technical Feasibility and Preliminary Assessment

We evaluate the technical feasibility of your new product, process, or investment ideas, conduct risk analyses, and provide engineering input to your decision-making processes.

## What We Don't Do

### <Close size={20} /> Financial Advisory and Accounting

We do not provide consultancy on accounting for R&D expenditures, cost calculations, financial statements, or tax declarations.

### <Close size={20} /> Tax Calculations and Incentive Applications

R&D deductions, income tax withholding incentives, social security premium support calculations, or tax optimization are not within our area of expertise.

### <Close size={20} /> Certified Public Accountant Reports and Auditing

We do not prepare certified public accountant attestation reports. We do not provide independent auditing or financial statement auditing services.

### <Close size={20} /> Legal Consultancy

We do not provide legal consultancy on intellectual property rights, contract law, labor law, or commercial law.

### <Close size={20} /> Human Resources Management

We do not establish personnel recruitment processes, payroll management, personnel affairs, or performance evaluation systems.

### <Close size={20} /> Production and Manufacturing Operations

We do not manage operational processes such as factory management, production planning operations, or supply chain management.
