# R&D Centers Statistics

---

## General Information

With Law No. 5746, it is aimed to increase our country's international competitiveness, develop its innovation capacity,
and establish an industrial infrastructure suitable for global developments by creating an economic environment focused
on innovation, with developed qualified employment, producing high value-added products, and with high efficiency and
competitiveness; and also to enable foreign capital companies to develop new technologies in our country by structuring
their R&D units in our country.

Private Sector R&D Centers are established and operate within the scope of Law No. 5746, which was prepared with the
understanding that investments made in R&D, technology and human resources return as technological advancement, high
competitiveness and high welfare level.

For detailed information, please visit <a href="http://agtm.sanayi.gov.tr" target="_blank" rel="noopener noreferrer">here</a>.
