# Center Setup Consultancy

Establishing an R&D or Design Center under Law No. 5746 is not merely about filling out
an application form. A genuine center requires properly defined projects, competent personnel
structure, suitable physical infrastructure, and a sustainable management system.

**Setup with engineering perspective, consultancy with academic assurance.**
