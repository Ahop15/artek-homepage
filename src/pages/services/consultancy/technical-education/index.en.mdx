# Technical Training

We share our academic expertise and industry experience with your organization's technical teams. We do not offer formal certification programs; our goal is knowledge transfer and practical competency development.

**Not certification, knowledge transfer. Not theory, practice.**

---

## Our Training Approach

As ARTEK, our training programs are based on practice-oriented knowledge transfer, drawing from our academic background and industry experience.

Key characteristics of our training:

- **Knowledge Transfer Focused:** Content that combines theoretical knowledge with practical applications, directly contributing to your business processes
- **Customized Programs:** Training programs designed according to your organization's sector, current competency level, and specific needs
- **Application-Oriented:** Workshop sessions conducted through your own projects and real-world scenarios
- **Mentoring Support:** Continuous communication opportunity where you can get answers to your questions even after the training

> **Note:** For training requiring formal certification (ISO certification, mandatory regulatory training, etc.), we provide referrals to authorized institutions.

## Our Training Formats

We offer flexible formats suitable for your organization's needs and working arrangements.

### In-House Training

Group training programs conducted at your organization for your entire team or specific departments. Hands-on learning opportunity in your own environment, working on your own projects.

### Workshop Sessions

Intensive and interactive working sessions focused on a specific topic or skill. Opportunity to gain hands-on experience working on real projects.

### One-on-One Mentoring

Personalized mentoring programs for project managers or technical leaders. Knowledge transfer at a flexible pace, focusing on specific challenges you face.

### Remote Training

Live training sessions conducted through online platforms. Participation opportunity with flexible scheduling, without geographical constraints.

### 1. Needs Analysis

We assess your organization's current competency level, goals, and training needs together. We identify which areas require development and set priorities.

### 2. Program Design

We design a customized training program based on the needs analysis. We consider your preferences regarding content, format, duration, and scheduling.

### 3. Training Implementation

We conduct training according to the established program. We reinforce theoretical knowledge with practical applications and work on real projects.

### 4. Evaluation and Follow-up

After training, we evaluate the outcomes and plan additional sessions if necessary. We provide mentoring support for your questions.
