# Turkey Design Centers Statistics

**Last Updated:** October 31, 2025
**Data Source:** Ministry of Industry and Technology of the Republic of Turkey, General Directorate of R&D Incentives
**Legal Basis:** Law No. 5746 on Supporting Research, Development and Design Activities
**Reform Package Publication Date:** February 26, 2016

## Overview

As of October 31, 2025, a total of 343 Design Centers are operating in Turkey. These centers employ 8,502 personnel and have completed 15,370 projects.

## Key Statistics

### General Information

The number of active Design Centers is 343.
The total number of Personnel is 8,502.
The number of Foreign/Foreign Partnership Companies with Design Centers is 41.

### Education Levels

Distribution of personnel by education level in Design centers:

The number of personnel with Bachelor's education is 5,506.
The number of personnel with Master's education is 801.
The number of personnel with Doctorate and above education is 51.

### Project and Patent Statistics

The number of completed projects is 15,370.
The number of ongoing projects is 2,452.
The total number of patents is 937.
The number of registered patents is 333.
The number of patent applications is 604.
