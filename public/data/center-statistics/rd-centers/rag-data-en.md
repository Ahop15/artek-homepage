# Turkey R&D Centers Statistics

**Last Updated:** October 31, 2025
**Data Source:** Ministry of Industry and Technology of the Republic of Turkey

## Overview

As of October 31, 2025, a total of 1.358 R&D centers are operating in Turkey. These centers employ 91.194 personnel and have completed 74.815 projects.

## Key Statistics

### General Information

The total number of operating R&D Centers is 1.358.
The total number of personnel including support staff is 91.194.
The number of R&D Centers with foreign ownership or foreign partnership is 242.

### Education Levels

Distribution of personnel by education level in R&D centers:

The number of personnel with Bachelor's degree education is 56.305.
The number of personnel with Master's degree education is 17.590.
The number of personnel with Doctorate and above education is 1.909.

### Project and Patent Statistics

The number of completed projects is 74.815.
The number of ongoing projects is 15.784.
The total number of patents is 45.045.
The number of registered patents is 16.001.
The number of patent applications is 28.871.
